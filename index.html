Ano nga ba ang magpapakita ng pagiging isang Tatak Pinoy?

This question lingered in my mind while designing this logo. After much brainstorming and research, I created this logo to represent the Tatak Pinoy Program. In the middle, we see a Filipino, in support of every Filipino industry and artisan. High above, a star ignites, a symbol of the exceptional quality of Filipino products and services. But it's more than just that. The rising steps depict our relentless efforts to enhance our cultural presence globally, and ultimately elevate our nation’s economic performance through our bright innovative ideas, represented by the bulb, and an arrow going up and sprouting on salakot signifies a sustainable economic future that blossoms from our vibrant spirit. The colors are carefully chosen to reflect the Philippines and the happiness we embody. This isn't just a logo; it's the essence of Tatak Pinoy, captured and ready to develop a “Proudly Filipino” brand to become more globally competitive.
